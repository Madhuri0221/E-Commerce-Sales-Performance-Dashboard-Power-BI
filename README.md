# Retail-Analytics-Dashboard-Power-BI
BI Visualisations

## Goal
Design and develop an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze sales, payments, customer experience, and regional performance, enabling strategic decision-making and improved visibility of business KPIs.

## Description
Worked on a retail marketplace dataset (Shop Nest Store, Portugal) containing 9 interconnected datasets covering sales, payments, orders, and customer ratings. Performed data cleaning, transformation, and modelling to prepare reliable inputs for analysis. Built interactive dashboards to highlight key performance metrics, including top 10 product categories by sales, delayed vs. on-time orders (~12% delayed), and monthly sales comparisons. Conducted state-wise and seasonal sales analysis to uncover regional and quarterly trends, and developed revenue visuals to track total revenue and year-over-year growth (~18%). Delivered actionable insights into sales patterns, payment method preferences, and customer behaviour.

Delivered a consolidated dashboard providing real-time, actionable insights into sales performance, operational bottlenecks, and customer behaviour, enabling better pricing, planning, and business strategies.
